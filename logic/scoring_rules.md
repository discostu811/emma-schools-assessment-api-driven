# Scoring Rules

## Weights
- Academics: 25%
- Fit: 20%
- Arts: 15%
- Environment/Pastoral: 15%
- Commute: 15%
- Reputation: 10%

## Score Definitions (1-5)
1 - Evidence indicates significant weaknesses or persistent negative trends.
2 - Limited strengths; notable concerns remain.
3 - Solid baseline performance with mixed signals.
4 - Consistently strong factual indicators.
5 - Exceptional, verifiable outcomes across recent years.

Detailed operationalization lives in `emma_schools/pipelines/scoring.py`.
