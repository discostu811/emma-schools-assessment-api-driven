# Source Universe for Raw Fact Research
_Last updated: 2025-11-07_

## Purpose
To ensure each school's **Raw Facts** file captures every relevant, publicly available, attributable source — official, third-party, and social — so the factual base is complete and reusable.

## Source Categories

### (3) Official & Institutional
- School website (prospectus, curriculum, policies, staff, term dates)
- Inspection reports (ISI, Ofsted)
- DfE performance tables and databases
- Charity Commission / Companies House filings
- Verified school social media: X (Twitter), Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook
- Annual reports, newsletters, head's letters
- Exam results published directly by school or DfE

### (3) High Reliability Third Parties
- Good Schools Guide, Tatler, School House Magazine
- Sunday Times Parent Power, TES, Times, Guardian, Telegraph, Independent
- Local press (Ealing Times, Chiswick Calendar, Richmond & Twickenham Times)
- League tables, academic outcomes, awards sites

### (2) Independent Media
- Educational blogs with named authors
- Substacks, identifiable YouTube reviews
- Fee databases (ISC Census, schoolfeeschecker)

### (1–2) Community & Social Commentary
- Mumsnet, Reddit (UK education/local), Tootle, Netmums
- Google Maps, Trustpilot, WhichSchoolAdvisor, Niche.com
- Twitter/X threads, TikTok/Instagram posts (attributable only)
- Facebook or alumni groups with identifiable authors
- Summarise patterns only when multiple sources align

### (2–3) Cultural & Outcome Data
- Press on arts/sport achievements, competitions, awards
- University destinations (UCAS, LinkedIn sampling)
- Partnerships, outreach, scholarships

### (2) Reference & Meta
- Wikipedia (cross-check only)
- LinkedIn school page (alumni and scale)
- Aggregators and education data APIs (for context)

## Reliability Codes
1 = forum/social  |  2 = news/blog  |  3 = official/report

## Inclusion
Publicly available, attributable, timestamped.

## Exclusion
Anonymous hearsay, unverifiable or AI-generated text, paywalled/private leaks.

Each `/raw/*.md` must include a `source-log` listing every source with access date and type.
